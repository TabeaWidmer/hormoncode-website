1. General Data Protection

The protection of your personal data is important to us. We treat personal data confidentially and in accordance with legal data protection regulations, particularly the Swiss Data Protection Act (DPA) and the principles of the GDPR.

2. Collection and Processing of Personal Data

We collect and process the following data when you use our website or app:

  • Personal Data: Name, email address, location (country/region)
  • Usage Data: Information about app/website usage, e.g., log data, cookies
  • Health Data: Only with your explicit consent and within the scope of app functionalities (symptoms, diary, wearables)
  • Communication Data: Messages, contact forms, support requests

Data is only used to the extent necessary for providing functions and services or with your explicit consent.

3. Purpose of Data Processing

We use the collected data for the following purposes:

  • Operation, maintenance and development of the app and website
  • Personalization and optimization of user experience
  • Creation of health reports and coach recommendations
  • Sending newsletters and important notifications (only with explicit consent)
  • Compliance with legal obligations and protection of user rights

4. Legal Basis for Processing

Data processing is based on:

  • Art. 6 para. 1 lit. a GDPR: User consent
  • Art. 6 para. 1 lit. b GDPR: Contract fulfillment (provision of app service)
  • Art. 6 para. 1 lit. c GDPR: Legal obligations
  • Swiss Data Protection Act (DPA)

5. Disclosure to Third Parties

Personal data is not sold or rented to third parties. Disclosure only occurs:

  • To service providers (e.g., hosting, payment processors, cloud services) who are contractually bound to data security
  • In case of legal obligation, e.g., authority requests
  • To B2B cooperation partners, only anonymized or pseudonymized summaries with opt-in

6. Data Transfer Abroad

Data is stored and processed within Switzerland and possibly in the EU (e.g., hosting in eu-central-1, eu-west-2). We ensure that an adequate level of data protection is guaranteed.

7. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website/app uses technically necessary cookies as well as optional analysis and tracking technologies only with your active consent (cookie banner).

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Information about your stored data
  • Correction, deletion or restriction of processing
  • Objection to processing
  • Data portability
  • Withdrawal of given consents at any time without disadvantages

To exercise your rights, you can contact us at any time at hello@hormoncode.com.

9. Data Security

We take appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, loss or misuse (encryption, access controls).

10. Changes to Privacy Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. The current version will always be published on the website.
